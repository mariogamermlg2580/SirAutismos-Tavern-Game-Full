Rules Table

RuleEffect
GoalGet rid of all cards. More than 25 cards forfeits.
Friend ModeTurns the table into 2-player local pass-and-play with you and a friend.
Card PlayMatch rank/suit like UNO, or play an 8 as wild.
Ace EffectsHearts: next player draws 3. Spades: your dice lock for 1 turn. Clubs: all players get 2 coins, you get 5. Diamonds: skip the next player.
Poker DiscardsTwo pair, three of a kind, full house, straight, and flush can be played.
Action CardsOptional rare randomized cards. Usually 1 per deck, sometimes 2. They cost 2 skipped turns and can trigger a free dice roll, discard cards, or cash in a free chip.
Free DiceOnly straights and flushes earn a free dice roll.
Dice CostNormal rolls draw half the die size. D12 unlocks on turn 10 and D20 unlocks on turn 20.
Dice TargetClick or tap an opponent panel during your turn. Big target draws are capped so one roll cannot instantly end the table.
ShopCards, darts, pool, and bowling each have different buyable items. Card table items use table coins; lounge games use table coins or a small bar wallet when played alone.
CosmeticsCard, table, and bowling cosmetics are visual only. Table skins include Cosmic, Void, Minecraft, Fire, and Galactic Emperor looks.
DealerOptional chaos event: 1%, then 15% after turn 6, then 50% past turn 8.
Deck RouletteGold adds coins. Dragon starts with 15 cards and no dice. Blue adds rumors. Orange starts at 5 cards, caps at 15, uses D4 only, and has no action cards. Strike is a black-suit speed deck with no coins or action cards. Royal, Mirror, Eclipse, Silver, Wildfire, Storm, Quiet, and Joker add stronger table twists.
BowlingStandard 10-frame bowling. Drag up the lane to roll. Shop items only help focus, spin, lane control, and cosmetics.
Win PayoutsCard wins pay by AI difficulty: Easy 1, Medium 3, Hard 6. Boss pass now costs 50 wins.
